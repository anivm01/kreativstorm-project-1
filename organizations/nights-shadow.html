The Night’s Shadow

The Night's Shadow is a prominent Thieves Guild that operates in the city of Solace. Unlike other guilds, they are not driven by greed, but by a strong desire to fight against the oppression of the Divine Council, which seeks to eliminate all forms of arcane and divine magic.

The Night's Shadow works closely with the Order of the Silver Star, a religious organization that seeks to protect divine magic practitioners and smuggle them out of the country. They provide essential support by smuggling goods and people, gathering intelligence, and carrying out clandestine operations. In exchange, the Order provides the guild with protection and financial support.

The Night's Shadow operates in secrecy, and their members are experts at infiltration and subterfuge. They often pose as ordinary citizens or merchants to avoid detection and gather information. The guild is extremely cautious and selective when it comes to recruiting new members, preferring to keep their operations small and tight-knit.

Despite their noble cause, the Night's Shadow is not averse to using their skills for personal gain. Some of their members may engage in illicit activities such as theft and smuggling, but they make sure not to compromise their alliance with the Order.

The alliance between the Night's Shadow and the Order of the Silver Star is a testament to the power of unlikely allies in the face of oppression. Together, they provide a formidable force against the Divine Council's tyranny, and give hope to those who seek to practice their faith without fear.
