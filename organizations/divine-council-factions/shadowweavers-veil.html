The Shadowweaver's Veil

Followers of Nyx, the Shadowweaver

The Shadowweaver's Veil, led by Nyx, the Shadowweaver, specialize in espionage, covert operations, and gathering intelligence. They ensure the protection of Celestia from internal and external threats, and maintain secrecy and vigilance in the face of hidden dangers.

They are a secretive and enigmatic group, dedicated to the art of deception and subterfuge. The Shadowweaver's Veil are skilled in the art of illusion and manipulation, and they often serve as spies and assassins. They are also responsible for maintaining the secrecy and security of Celestia, ensuring that no external threats can penetrate its borders.
