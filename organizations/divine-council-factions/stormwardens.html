The Stormwardens

Followers of Raijin, the Stormbringer

The Stormwardens, followers of Raijin, the Stormbringer, are masters of weather manipulation, disaster mitigation, and agriculture. They advise on matters related to weather patterns, agriculture, and natural disasters, ensuring that the realm is well-prepared for the forces of nature.

They are a proud and adventurous group, dedicated to harnessing the power of nature and the elements. The Stormwardens are skilled in the art of weather manipulation and elemental magic, and they often serve as explorers and scouts. They are also responsible for maintaining the weather patterns of Celestia, ensuring that crops grow and that the land remains fertile.
