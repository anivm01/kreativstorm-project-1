The Valiant Vanguard

Followers of Kharn, the Battlefather

The Valiant Vanguard, followers of Kharn, the Battlefather, are experts in matters of defense, warfare, and strategy. They advise on matters of national security, defense against external threats, and maintaining the honor and integrity of Celestia through valor and courage.

They are a disciplined and formidable group of warriors, dedicated to protecting Celestia from external threats. The Valiant Vanguard are known for their unwavering loyalty and bravery, and they have a strict code of honor that they abide by. They are also responsible for training the next generation of warriors and maintaining the military strength of Celestia.
