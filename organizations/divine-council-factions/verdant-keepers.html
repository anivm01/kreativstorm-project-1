The Verdant Keepers

Followers of Elara, the Green Guardian

The Verdant Keepers, are experts in matters of nature, wildlife, and environmental conservation. They ensure the protection and preservation of Celestia's natural resources, advocate for sustainable practices, and maintain the balance between civilization and nature.

They are a peaceful and nurturing group, dedicated to preserving the natural beauty of Celestia. The Verdant Keepers are skilled in the art of herbalism and gardening, and they often serve as healers and caretakers. They are also responsible for maintaining the balance of nature in Celestia, ensuring that its flora and fauna remain healthy and vibrant.
