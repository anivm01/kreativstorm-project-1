The Radiant League

The Radiant League is a legally sanctioned adventurer's guild in Celestia that serves as a hub for quests, missions, and employment opportunities for adventurers of all kinds. The League has a reputation for being a reliable source of skilled and competent individuals who can handle a wide variety of tasks and challenges.

As a legally sanctioned organization, the Radiant League is subject to strict regulations from the Divine Council, and all of its members are required to follow a code of conduct that emphasizes lawful behavior and adherence to the principles of the divine deities. Despite these regulations, the League is well-respected and trusted by both the general public and the ruling authorities.

The Radiant League provides a variety of services to its members, including access to job boards, training facilities, and equipment stores. It also offers a variety of support services, such as legal representation and medical care. Members of the League are expected to pay dues and follow the regulations set forth by the Divine Council.

Despite the restrictions imposed by the Divine Council, the Radiant League remains a vital organization for adventurers seeking work and opportunities for adventure. Its reputation for reliability and professionalism has made it a respected and influential force in Celestia, and its members are known for their commitment to upholding the ideals of the divine deities.

Among the jobs posted on the Radiant League job boards there are a lot of contracts for hunting down known arcane magic users.
