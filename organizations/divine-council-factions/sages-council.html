The Sage's Council

Followers of Sylvaris, the Wise Sage

The Sage's Council, followers of Sylvaris, the Wise Sage, are scholars and advisers known for their wisdom, knowledge, and enlightenment. They provide counsel on matters of history, philosophy, and governance, and ensure that the realm is guided by wisdom and insight.

They are a scholarly and introspective group, dedicated to the pursuit of knowledge, wisdom, and enlightenment. The members of The Sage's Council are known for their keen intellect and deep understanding of various subjects, including history, philosophy, and the natural world. They are skilled in divination, prophecy, and ancient lore, and they often serve as advisors to the rulers of Celestia, providing counsel and insight on matters of governance and decision-making. The Sage's Council is also responsible for preserving and expanding the knowledge and wisdom of Celestia, ensuring that its citizens have access to education and enlightenment to help guide them on their paths. They are held in high esteem by the other factions for their wisdom and impartiality, and they are often sought after for their guidance and counsel in times of need.
