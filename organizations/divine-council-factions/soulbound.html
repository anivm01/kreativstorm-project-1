The Soulbound

Followers of Morwyn, the Reaper of Souls

The Soulbound, led by Morwyn, the Reaper of Souls, offer insights on matters related to the afterlife, death, and the souls of the departed. They provide guidance on matters of justice, punishment, and redemption, and ensure that the souls of the departed find their rightful place in the cycle of life and death.

They are a solemn and introspective group, dedicated to understanding the mysteries of death and the afterlife. They are responsible for conducting funerary rites and ensuring that the souls of the deceased make their way safely to the underworld. The Soulbound are skilled in divination and the art of communing with spirits, and they often serve as advisors to rulers and leaders.
