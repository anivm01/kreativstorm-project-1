The Blossom's Embrace

Followers of Auriel, The Eternal Blossom

The Blossom's Embrace are experts in matters of growth, rejuvenation, and fertility. They advise on matters of agriculture, horticulture, and fertility rituals, ensuring bountiful harvests and prosperity for the realm.

They are a creative and expressive group, dedicated to fostering art, culture, and beauty in Celestia. The Blossom's Embrace are skilled in the art of music, dance, and storytelling, and they often serve as entertainers and teachers. They are also responsible for maintaining the artistic and cultural richness of Celestia, ensuring that its citizens have access to the beauty and creativity that enriches their lives.
