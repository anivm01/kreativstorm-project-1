Raijin, The Stormbringer

God of the Tempest Domain

Raijin, the Stormbringer, is a powerful and revered deity associated with storms, lightning, and thunder in the world of Celestia. He is often depicted as a towering figure clad in armor made of storm clouds, wielding a mighty hammer or drum that he uses to create and control thunderstorms.

In some cultures, Raijin is also seen as a protector of the weak and a punisher of evil, with his thunderbolts serving as a symbol of divine justice.
