The Circle of Elders

The Circle of Elders, comprising representatives from each of the eight factions that follow the major deities in Celestia, serves as the ruling body of the realm. The Circle convenes to make decisions on matters of governance, policy, and law, guided by the principles and teachings of the deities they represent. Each faction brings its unique perspectives, expertise, and influence to the Circle, contributing to the overall governance and wellbeing of Celestia.

Together, the leaders of these eight factions form the Circle of Elders, bringing their unique expertise and perspectives to the table while upholding the beliefs and principles of their respective deities. The Council works in harmony to govern Celestia and ensure the welfare and prosperity of its people, upholding the prohibition on arcane magic while utilizing their diverse skills, knowledge, and abilities to maintain the balance and harmony of the realm.
